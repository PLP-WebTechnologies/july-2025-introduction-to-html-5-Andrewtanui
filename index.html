About Drake

Drake's real name is Aubrey Drake Graham. He was born on October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Canada. Before becoming a rapper, he was an actor on a TV show called "Degrassi."

Drake started making music in 2006. He became very famous with his mixtape "So Far Gone" in 2009. Now he is one of the most popular rappers in the world.
